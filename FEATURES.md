- ✅ **Pandit Search**: Find pandits by location, rating, specialization
- ✅ **Advanced Booking**:

  - Select number of participants
  - Add preferences (vegetarian, language, etc.)
- ✅ **Booking History**: View all past and upcoming bookings
- ✅ **Booking Management**:

- ✅ **Real-time Updates**: Socket.IO notifications for booking status
